What is Magazine & Brochure Design?

Our Design Process
#1 First Meeting
We set up a meeting with you that helps us get to know each other, understand our goals and define a way for us to work together.
#2 Design & Delivery
We share a quote and after we agree on terms we start working on the design phase & iterate on changes or suggestion you might have and finally deliver a design we are all satisfied with !
