What is Product Design?
A process of creating a product for the right target users by understanding their needs and identifying pain points to provide great user experiences that solves their problems

Our Product Design Process
#1 First Meeting
We set up a meeting with you that helps us get to know each other, understand our goals and define a way for us to work together.
#2 Roadmap Planning
We create a roadmap or structure of product design phase and share it with you letting you know which of following phase will take how long and understand and confirm the compensation as well .
#3 Design, Prototype, Iterate
After we propose the Roadmap, once the design is confirmed we begin working on the requirements inside the timframes we both agreed upon and according to the plans iterate over it.
#4 Testing & Support
As we complete our design phase which satisifies your needs, we help you perform testing with potential user base regarding the design to make sure it's functioning as intended .
